As hinted at by "Spark's units" in the flavortext and the "SI" initials of this puzzle, this is a puzzle about electrical SI units. Additionally, "mixing in" clues exactly how they're incorporated into this puzzle: names of electrical SI units can be found anagrammed within the answers. This is most obvious with pairs like the WATT in FLOW STATE or the VOLT in SURVIVAL TOOLS.

After finding a few such pairs, solvers might also notice that the anagrammed unit is never a contiguous substring within the feeder answer; rather, there's always exactly one extra letter within the letters of the anagrammed unit. This provides an extraction of one letter per answer. Finally, the section of the feeder answer comprising the anagrammed unit and the extra letter always starts at a different position within the answer, providing an ordering.

The puzzle answer, BLAST AC, summarizes the advice given to Blanche: besides turning Mystic's own AC up to chill out, she should blast Instinct's AC supply away!

Author’s Notes

This puzzle originally had a different sorting step, with the first letters of answers being the common variable representations of each of the involved units, like I for current/amperes. This proved tricky in testsolving, since the easiest way to get the answer was almost always just to extract the letters and blindly anagram them. We tried inserting stronger and stronger hints into the flavortext to aid the realization, but in the end we decided it would just be cleaner to use a generic ordering that solvers were more likely to get, to reduce the incentive to short-circuit the puzzle and also allow us to trim down the flavortext to its current state.
