Solution: Overdone
Answer: YEARNING

Written by Level 51

This puzzle is about mangled clues. Solvers are given eight mangled clues; as is typical of the genre, we can figure out the way each word is transformed, and solve the original clues:

Mangled clue: ABLECRAF MI AFTSMINE STRYGEND DM DU NTERIMPR SIDEALON ARELAB
Transformation: remove the central letter or letters, and swap the two remaining halves around
Original clue: CRAFTABLE ITEM IN MINECRAFTS GENDUSTRY MOD USED IN AN IMPRINTER ALONGSIDE LABWARE → GENETIC TEMPLATE

Mangled clue: ALB ERENNIAB RASI ROF ROWD OMMERCIALLO OH UCROSU
Transformation: shift the last letter by -10, and delete the first letter
Original clue: TALL PERENNIAL GRASS CROP GROWN COMMERCIALLY FOR SUCROSE → SUGARCANE

Mangled clue: CPIM YJOS VKTL KU UQMETIME VTANSLATE CU IJOSTOR
Transformation: shift each of the first two letters by +2, and delete the last letter
Original clue: ANIME WHOSE TITLE IS SOMETIMES TRANSLATED AS GHOSTORY → BAKEMONOGATARI

Mangled clue: ESRISTANCE NIUTS ERPHAPS
Transformation: move the first letter into the third position
Original clue: RESISTANCE UNITS PERHAPS → OHMS

Mangled clue: SWT WDROW KNIDECERP VONOH VO WNAELRO
Transformation: shift the last letter by +4, then reverse
Original clue: TWO WORDS PRECEDING HONOR OR ORLEANS → MAID OF

Mangled clue: THREJ WORH EXPRFSIOR MEANJK TS WASTJ TIMI CREAUNK SOMEUINK ALREBC IR EXISUNCI
Transformation: shift the fifth letter by +1, then the last letter by +4, then delete the sixth letter
Original clue: THREE WORD EXPRESSION MEANING TO WASTE TIME CREATING SOMETHING ALREADY IN EXISTENCE → REINVENT THE WHEEL

Mangled clue: TOKO RAI STAION SEQENTALL BETEEN HINDE AND ODABAKIHIKOE
Transformation: delete every fourth letter
Original clue: TOKYO RAIL STATION SEQUENTIALLY BETWEEN HINODE AND ODAIBAKAIHINKOEN → SHIBAURA-FUTO

Mangled clue: UL AM NGLIS ILLAG OM H OYA ORSESHOE N
Transformation: delete the first and last letters
Original clue: FULL NAME OF ENGLISH VILLAGE HOME TO THE ROYAL HORSESHOES INN → WALTHAM ON THE WOLDS

The usual next step in mangled clues puzzles is to re-encode the resultant answers into something of note. However, re-encoding our answers doesn't seem to give anything useful.

Instead, solvers need to realize that they must perform the re-encoding step multiple times in order to get answers that they can use. Some possible ways solvers might figure this out are by noticing that transforming OHMS once gives HMOS, which is just one step away from the suspiciously puzzly MOHS, or perhaps by interpreting the title creatively. Regardless, transforming some clues should confirm this idea. After some number of transformations, each answer turns into a recognizable word.

Original answer Transforms Post-transformations
GENETIC TEMPLATE 5 PLANET
SUGARCANE 3 ARCANA
BAKEMONOGATARI 7 POKEMON
OHMS 2 MOHS
MAID OF 9 ZODIAC
REINVENT THE WHEEL 8 REINDEER
SHIBAURA-FUTO 4 SHIFT
WALTHAM ON THE WOLDS 6 MONTH

Two observations can be made here: first, each final answer represents an ordered set of things, so we can take the n-th item of that set, where we made n transformations to the original answer. Secondly, the transforms are consecutive numbers from 2 to 9, which also provides an ordering on our items.

Finally, we can apply the indices at the bottom of the puzzle to index into our items for the final answer YEARNING.

Author’s Notes

While writing for this hunt, I spent a while deliberately thinking about well-established puzzle types and how I could put a fresh spin on them; the results were this puzzle and Cyclic. I wasn't super sure whether this idea would be interesting or feasible at first, but finding the transformation from BAKEMONOGATARI to POKEMON convinced me to go all the way with it. REINVENT THE WHEEL to REINDEER was a complete stroke of luck that resulted from me going through lots of phrases that shared prefixes with REINDEER, and shows that with the power of Caesar shifts, anything is possible.
