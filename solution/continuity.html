Author’s Notes

I wrote this puzzle about a week before hunt, thinking of how a shape might appear to move in a continuous path if it were depicted in positions in sequential "slides" by brainstorming off the answer, and then adding vaguely thematic mechanics until it seemed reasonable. In hindsight, the first Hidato step is probably underclued for an intro round puzzle and I might have considered adding an example or other clue to how it worked. The puzzle is fairly cheeseable after the Hidatos — my first testsolver skipped both ordering steps and just solved the puzzle by moving columns around until he noticed something and then anagramming the continuation letters; but I figured it was good enough for an intro round puzzle.
